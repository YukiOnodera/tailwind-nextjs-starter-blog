# はじめに

**stimulate** という単語はどのように使うのでしょうか？

## 例文

- The production of melanin in the skin **is stimulated** by the sun.
- That lecture really **stimulated me**.
- I'm trying to stimulate him to study harder.

## その他

- That movie is stimulating and exciting.
